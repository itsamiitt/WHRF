Overview

Networking Solutions

A reliable and secure network is the backbone of modern business operations. WRHWFOUR designs, deploys, and maintains enterprise-grade networking solutions that ensure seamless connectivity, optimal performance, and robust security across your entire organization — from a single office to multi-site operations spanning the country.

Our network engineers are certified in Cisco, Juniper, Fortinet, and Ubiquiti technologies. We specialize in structured cabling (Cat6/Cat6A/Fiber), enterprise firewall configuration, VPN deployment, wireless network planning, SD-WAN implementations, and comprehensive network security assessments.

How It Works

Our Process

A structured, transparent approach to building reliable network infrastructure.

Network Assessment & Survey

Survey your premises, analyze bandwidth requirements, map coverage zones, and design the optimal network topology for your current and future needs.

Architecture Design

Create detailed network architecture documents, structured cabling plans, equipment specifications, IP addressing schemes, and VLAN segmentation strategies.

Deployment & Configuration

Install structured cabling, switches, routers, firewalls, and access points. Configure VLANs, DHCP, DNS, VPN, QoS, and security policies.

Testing & Handover

Comprehensive performance testing, bandwidth verification, security auditing, and complete documentation handover with staff training.
